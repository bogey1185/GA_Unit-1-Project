How to Play

Players take turns rolling dice to earn points. Each turn begins with 6 dice. If the player's initial roll does not score any points, he or she has 'FARKLED', and the turn is over. If the player does roll dice that are worth points, the player must choose the dice to keep. The points associated with the chosen dice are banked in the 'turn score'. The player may then choose to roll again or end the turn.

If the player ends the turn, the points in the turn score are permanently added to the leaderboard. If, however, the player rolls again, he or she will only roll the dice that were not previously scored.

If the player farkles, his or her turn is over, and all points in the turn score bank are forfeited.

On the other hand, if the player continues to roll scoring dice, he or she will continue to select dice to keep each roll until all 6 dice are scored. In that case, the player has 'hot dice' and he or she may roll again with all 6 dice. Play continues in the aforementioned fashion until the player farkles or ends his or her turn.

The first player to reach 10,000 points on the leaderboard wins!
