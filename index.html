How to Play

Players take turns rolling dice to earn points. After each roll, the player may select the dice that will be scored. To select dice, simply click on the dice in the lower row to move them to the top scoring row. Upon clicking the roll or end turn buttons, the dice will be automatically scored, and the score will be added to the turn score.

After scoring, the scored dice are removed from the game. The player may choose to roll the remaining dice, or end his or her turn. If the player ends the turn, any points in the turn score will be added to the leaderboard. If the player rolls, he or she will have an opportunity to score further points. If the player scores with all six dice, the player has "hot dice" and may start again with six dice.

A player farkles when his or her roll does not produce any scoreable dice. A player also farkles if he or she attempts to score dice that are not part of a scoreable combination. If a player farkles, any points in the turn score are forfeited, and the turn is over.

Each turn, a player continues to roll until he or she ends the turn voluntarily or farkles. The first player to reach 10,000 points on the leaderboard wins!
